Welcome to Stockify.



Stockify started in 2022 as a side project developed for NUS-ISS's Server Side Foundation module.

Today we are a standalone application and trusted by many investors around the world.

Stockify is the best way to find out what is happening right now around the markets, companies and assets you care about.

We continue to reimagine financial media, tools and services for the next generation of investors & traders.

Utilize our free data aggregation services to make your next investment moves.






Our Mission | To Stockify The World

To power the global conversation for investors & traders of all levels in order to connect, learn, profit and have fun.
