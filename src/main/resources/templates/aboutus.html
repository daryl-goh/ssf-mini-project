Welcome to Stockify.



Stockify started in 2022 as a side project developed for NUS-ISS's Server Side Foundation module.

Today we are a standalone application and trusted by millions of investors around the world.

Stockify is the best way to find out what is happening right now around the markets, companies and assets you care about.

We continue to reimagine financial media, tools and services for the next generation of investors & traders.
